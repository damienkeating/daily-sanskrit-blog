## Sanskrit news
- संवत् ६७१ जपानस्य सम्राट् तेनजी रोकोकु-नामकं जलघटीयन्त्रं प्रवेशयत्।
- तत् यन्त्रं समयमापनार्थं निर्मितं ओत्सु-नगरे स्थापितम्।
- अनेन प्राचीनपूर्वदेशीयकालमापनविद्याया विकासः स्पष्टः अभवत्।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Timekeeping
- घटी (ghaṭī) — water clock; literally "water pot". [water clock / 水钟]
- समयः (samayaḥ) — time; literally "proper or appointed moment". [time / 时间]
- यन्त्रम् (yantram) — instrument; literally "device for holding or mounting". [instrument / 仪器]

## English
- In 671, Emperor Tenji of Japan introduced a water clock named Rokoku to measure time and indicate hours.
- The instrument was placed in the capital city of Ōtsu as a mark of imperial innovation.
- This event highlights the early adoption of advanced timekeeping technology in ancient East Asia.

## Chinese
- 公元671年，日本天智天皇引入了名为“漏刻”（Rokoku）的水钟来测量时间和指示时辰。
- 这一仪器被安置在首都大津，象征着皇室的技术创新。
- 这一事件凸显了古代东亚地区对先进计时技术的早期采用。
