## Sanskrit news
- संवत्सरे ४३१ ख्रीष्टाब्दे एफेससभा आरब्धा, या तृतीया विश्वसभा आसीत्।
- अस्यां सभायां नेस्टोरियमते विरुद्धं धार्मिकनिर्णयः कृतः।
- एषा घटना ख्रीष्टधर्मस्य इतिहासे मतवादानां महत्त्वपूर्णं मोडम् अभवत्।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Ecclesiastical Council and Doctrine (धार्मिकसभा मतञ्च)
- सभा (sabhā) — council; assembly for deliberation or judgment. 会议 / council
- मतम् (matam) — doctrine, belief, tenet; a held opinion. 教义 / doctrine
- निर्णयः (nirṇayaḥ) — decision, verdict, resolution; definitive conclusion. 决议 / decision

## English
- In 431, the Council of Ephesus—the third ecumenical council—convened to address Nestorianism.
- The council condemned Nestorius's teachings and affirmed Mary as Theotokos, shaping orthodox Christology.
- This gathering became a decisive moment in defining Christian doctrine and centralizing ecclesiastical authority.

## Chinese
- 公元431年，以弗所会议——第三次大公会议——召开，旨在应对聂斯脱里主义。
- 会议谴责了聂斯脱里的教义，确认了玛利亚“天主之母”的称号，确立了正统基督论。
- 这次集会成为定义基督教教义和集中教会权威的决定性时刻。
