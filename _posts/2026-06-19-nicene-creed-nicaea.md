**Daily Sanskrit learning - history's today: 19/06/325**

**Today's theme:** Council and Creed / 会议与信条

## Sanskrit news
- संवत् ३२५ तमे वर्षे जून मासस्य १९ दिनाङ्के निकैयायां धर्मसभायां मूलं विश्वासपत्रं स्वीकृतम् अभवत्।
- सम्राट् कॉन्स्टेन्टाइन्-महाना तत् सभा समावहत् येन ईसाई-समुदाये एकता रक्षिता स्यात्।
- तस्य निश्चयस्य घोषणा धार्मिकस्य जगतः मूलाधारं जातं यत् अद्यापि पठ्यते।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Council and Creed
- **सभा** (sabhā) — council, assembly; literally "gathering, meeting place". council / 议会
- **विश्वासः** (viśvāsaḥ) — faith, belief, creed; literally "trust, confidence". creed / 信条
- **निश्चयः** (niścayaḥ) — conviction, determination, fixed belief; literally "settling down, decision". conviction / 信念

## English
- On June 19, 325, the First Council of Nicaea adopted the original Nicene Creed, defining core Christian doctrine.
- Emperor Constantine convened the council to unify the Christian church and resolve theological disputes.
- This creed became a foundational statement of faith, still recited in churches worldwide today.

## Chinese
- 公元325年6月19日，第一次尼西亚会议通过了《尼西亚信经》原始文本，确立了基督教核心教义。
- 君士坦丁大帝召集此次会议，旨在统一基督教会并解决神学争端。
- 这一信经成为信仰的基石，至今仍在全球教会中被诵读。
