Daily Sanskrit learning - history's today: 21/05/1674
Today's theme: Governance / Election / 治理与选举

Sanskrit news:
- सन १६७४ तमस्य वर्षस्य मे २१ दिनाङ्के पोलैण्डदेशस्य कुलीनैः जॉन सोबिएस्की राजा इति निर्वाचितः। (On May 21, 1674, John Sobieski was elected King by the nobles of Poland.)
- सः लिथुएनियायाः महाप्रभुः अपि अभवत्, येन साम्राज्यस्य शक्तिः वर्धिता। (He also became Grand Duke of Lithuania, by which the empire's power increased.)
- तस्य राज्ये यूरोपीयदेशानां सुरक्षायै महान् योगदानं आसीत्। (In his reign there was great contribution to the security of European nations.)

Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Governance / Election
- राजा (rājā) — King; literal sense "ruler, sovereign". king / 国王
- निर्वाचनम् (nirvācanam) — Election; literal sense "selection, proclamation". election / 选举
- कुलीनः (kulīnaḥ) — Noble; literal sense "high-born, of good family". noble / 贵族

English
- On May 21, 1674, the nobility of Poland elected John Sobieski as King and Grand Duke of Lithuania.
- His election strengthened the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth during a period of intense external threats.
- Sobieski's reign became celebrated for his military leadership, especially his decisive victory at the Battle of Vienna in 1683.

Chinese
- 1674年5月21日，波兰贵族选举约翰·索别斯基为波兰国王和立陶宛大公。
- 他的当选加强了面临外部威胁的波兰立陶宛联邦。
- 索别斯基的统治以其军事领导而闻名，尤其是1683年维也纳战役的决定性胜利。
