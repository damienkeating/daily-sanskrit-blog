## Sanskrit news
- संवत् १११० इति वर्षे बाल्ड्विनः प्रथमो जीनोवीयनौसेनया सह बेरूतं नगरं अजयत्।
- फातिमीयखिलाफतस्य सैनिकाः पराजिताः अभवन्।
- एषः विजयः क्रूसेयुद्धकालीनसाम्राज्यविस्तारस्य प्रतीकः आसीत्।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Naval Conquest
- नौसेना (nausenā) — navy, fleet; literally "ship-army". [fleet / 舰队]
- विजयः (vijayaḥ) — victory, conquest; literally "triumph in battle". [victory / 胜利]
- आक्रमणम् (ākramaṇam) — attack, assault; literally "hostile invasion". [assault / 进攻]

## English
- In 1110, Baldwin I of Jerusalem captured the city of Beirut from the Fatimid Caliphate with the aid of a Genoese fleet.
- The victory demonstrated the growing naval power of Italian maritime republics in Mediterranean warfare during the Crusades.
- This conquest expanded the Kingdom of Jerusalem's territory and strengthened Crusader control over the Levantine coast.
