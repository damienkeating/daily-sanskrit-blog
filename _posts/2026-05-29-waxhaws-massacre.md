## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: War and Betrayal
- **युद्धम्** (yuddham) — war; literally "that which is fought". war / 战争
- **नरसंहारः** (narasaṃhāraḥ) — massacre; literally "slaughter of men, destruction of people". massacre / 屠杀
- **विश्वासघातः** (viśvāsaghātaḥ) — betrayal; literally "breaking of trust, breach of faith". betrayal / 背叛

## English
- On May 29, 1780, during the American Revolutionary War, British troops attacked Continental forces at Waxhaws, South Carolina, even as they attempted to surrender.
- The British continued their assault after the Continentals laid down their arms, killing 113 and critically wounding all but 53 of those who remained.
- This brutal incident became known as the Waxhaws Massacre and intensified colonial hostility toward the British army.

## Chinese
- 1780年5月29日，美国独立战争期间，英军在南卡罗来纳州沃克斯豪斯袭击了试图投降的大陆军。
- 大陆军放下武器后，英军仍继续进攻，造成113人死亡，幸存者中除53人外全部重伤。
- 这一残暴事件被称为沃克斯豪斯大屠杀，加剧了殖民地人民对英军的仇恨。
