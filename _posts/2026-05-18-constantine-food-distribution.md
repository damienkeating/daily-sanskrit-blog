## Daily Sanskrit learning - history's today: 18/05/332

**Today's theme: Charity and Governance / 慈善与治理**

### Sanskrit news
- वर्षे ३३२, रोमनसम्राट् कॉन्स्टेन्टाइन् महान् कॉन्स्टेन्टिनोपलनगरे नागरिकेभ्यः अन्नदानस्य घोषणामकरोत्।
- एतत् दानं सम्राज्ये शासकस्य प्रजासु दयां दर्शयति।
- प्रजानां कल्याणाय राज्ञः दायित्वं महत् अस्ति।

### Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Charity and Governance
- सम्राट् (samrāṭ) — Emperor; one who rules over all. emperor / 皇帝
- दानम् (dānam) — Gift, charity, distribution; that which is given. donation / 施舍
- नागरिकः (nāgarikaḥ) — Citizen; dweller of a city. citizen / 公民

### English
- In 332 AD, Emperor Constantine the Great announced free distributions of food to the citizens of Constantinople.
- This act revealed the emperor's responsibility toward the welfare of his subjects.
- Such charity strengthened the bond between the ruler and the people.

### Chinese
- 公元332年，君士坦丁大帝宣布向君士坦丁堡市民免费发放食物。
- 此举显示了皇帝对其臣民福祉的责任。
- 这种慈善加强了统治者与人民之间的联系。
