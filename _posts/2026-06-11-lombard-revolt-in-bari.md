## Sanskrit news
- संवत्सरे १०११ तमे जून-मासस्य एकादशे दिवसे बारी-नगरे ग्रीक-प्रजा लोम्बार्ड-विद्रोहिणो मेलु-नेतृन् विरुद्धम् उत्थिताः।
- तैः पराजित्य नगरं बैजन्तीय-शासकाय बासिल-मेसार्डोनिते समर्पितम्।
- अनेन बैजन्तीय-साम्राज्यस्य इताली-प्रदेशे प्रभुत्वं पुनः स्थिरीकृतम्।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Revolt and Governance
- विद्रोहः (vidrohaḥ) — revolt, rebellion; literally "breaking asunder". [revolt / 叛乱]
- नगरम् (nagaram) — city; literally "that which does not move". [city / 城市]
- शासकः (śāsakaḥ) — governor, ruler; literally "one who commands". [governor / 总督]

## English
- On June 11, 1011, Greek citizens of Bari rose up against the Lombard rebels led by Melus.
- After defeating the insurgents, they delivered the city to Basil Mesardonites, the Byzantine governor (catepan) of Italy.
- This event restored Byzantine control over Bari and suppressed the Lombard revolt in southern Italy.
