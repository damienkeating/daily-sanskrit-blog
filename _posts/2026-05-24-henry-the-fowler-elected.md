Daily Sanskrit learning - history's today: 24/05/919
Today's theme: Governance and Leadership / 治理与领导

Sanskrit news:
- ९१९ वर्षे फ्रान्कोनिया-सैक्सोनीय-कुलीनैः फ्रिट्ज्लार् सभायां हेन्री राजा निर्वाचितः।
- पूर्वीय-फ्राङ्क-राज्यस्य नेता वैशिष्ट्येण चुनीतः।

Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Governance and Leadership
- राजा (rājā) — king; one who shines/protects. king / 国王
- निर्वाचनम् (nirvācanam) — election; selection. election / 选举
- सभा (sabhā) — assembly, council; gathering. assembly / 集会

English
- In 919, the nobles of Franconia and Saxony elected Henry the Fowler as king of East Francia at the Imperial Diet in Fritzlar.
- This election marked the consolidation of German tribal duchies under a single ruler after the collapse of Carolingian power.
- It laid the foundation for the future Holy Roman Empire and a new era of centralized kingship.

Chinese
- 公元919年，法兰克尼亚和萨克森的贵族在弗里茨拉尔帝国议会上选举亨利一世为东法兰克国王。
- 这次选举标志着加洛林王朝崩溃后日耳曼部落公国在单一统治者下的整合。
- 它为未来的神圣罗马帝国和中央集权君主制的新时代奠定了基础。
