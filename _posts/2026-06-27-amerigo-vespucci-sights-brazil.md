Daily Sanskrit learning - history's today: 27/06/1499
Today's theme: Exploration and Navigation / 探索与航海

Sanskrit news:
- सन १४९९ तमे वर्षे जूनमासस्य सप्तविंशे दिने आमेरिगो वेस्पुच्चिः ब्राजीलदेशस्य तटं ददर्श । (In the year 1499, on the 27th day of June, Amerigo Vespucci sighted the coast of Brazil.)
- समुद्रमार्गेण नवीनभूमेर्विषये ज्ञानं प्रसृतं जातम् । (Through the ocean route, knowledge about new lands spread.)
- तस्य दर्शनं पश्चिमजगतस्य सम्पर्के महत्यः परिवर्तनानि आनयत् । (His sighting brought great changes in the contact between worlds.)

Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Exploration and Navigation
- नाविकः (nāvikaḥ) — navigator, sailor; literal sense "one who operates a boat". navigator/水手
- समुद्रः (samudraḥ) — ocean; literal sense "gathering of waters". ocean/海洋
- दर्शनम् (darśanam) — sighting, vision; literal sense "act of seeing". sighting/发现

English:
- On June 27, 1499, Amerigo Vespucci sighted the coast of what is now Amapá State in Brazil during his transatlantic voyage.
- This oceanic expedition expanded European geographic knowledge and opened new maritime routes to the Americas.
- Vespucci's sighting marked a pivotal moment in the Age of Discovery, forever changing the course of global exploration.

Chinese:
- 1499年6月27日，亚美利哥·韦斯普奇在横渡大西洋的航行中，首次看到了今巴西北阿马帕州的海岸。
- 这次海洋探险扩展了欧洲的地理知识，并开辟了通往美洲的新海上航线。
- 韦斯普奇的这一发现标志着大航海时代的关键时刻，永远改变了全球探索的进程。
