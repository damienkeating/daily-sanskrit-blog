Daily Sanskrit learning - history's today: 22/05/1846
Today's theme: News & Cooperation / 新闻与合作

Sanskrit news:
- सन् १८४६ तमे वर्षे मे २२ दिनाङ्के न्यूयॉर्कनगरे समाचारसहकारिता स्थापिता। (On May 22, 1846, a news cooperative was established in New York City.)
- अस्याः संस्थायाः नाम एसोसिएटेड् प्रेस् इति आसीत्, या निरपेक्षवार्ताप्रसारणाय उद्दिष्टा। (The name of this institution was Associated Press, which was aimed at impartial news dissemination.)
- एतत् घटनम् विश्वस्य समाचारक्षेत्रे महत्त्वपूर्णम् परिवर्तनम् अकरोत्। (This event brought an important change in the world's news field.)

Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: News & Cooperation
- वार्ता (vārtā) — news, report; literal sense "that which is spoken or told". news / 新闻
- सहकारिता (sahakāritā) — cooperation, collaboration; literal sense "acting together". cooperation / 合作
- लेखनम् (lekhanam) — writing, composition; literal sense "the act of inscribing". writing / 写作

English
- On May 22, 1846, the Associated Press was founded in New York City as a non-profit news cooperative.
- Its mission was to gather and share news reports impartially among member newspapers.
- This event marked a turning point in the history of organized journalism worldwide.

Chinese
- 1846年5月22日，美联社在纽约市成立，成为一个非营利性新闻合作组织。
- 它的使命是在成员报纸之间公正地收集和分享新闻报道。
- 这一事件标志着全球有组织新闻业历史的一个转折点。
