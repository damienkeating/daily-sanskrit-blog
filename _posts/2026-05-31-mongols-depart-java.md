**Daily Sanskrit learning - history's today: 31/05/1293**

**Today's theme:** Invasion and Retreat / 入侵与撤退

## Sanskrit news
- सं १२९३ तमे वर्षे मङ्गोलाः जावात् प्रस्थानं कृतवन्तः।
- राजा कीर्तनेगरस्य सेनया तेषां आक्रमणं विफलम् अभवत्।
- अतः सिङ्घसारि-साम्राज्यस्य रक्षणं सफलम् अभवत्।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Invasion and Retreat
- आक्रमणम् (ākramaṇam) — invasion, attack; literally "stepping upon, assailing". [invasion / 侵略]
- पराजयः (parājayaḥ) — defeat; literally "being completely conquered (by another)". [defeat / 失败]
- प्रस्थानम् (prasthānam) — departure, retreat; literally "setting forth, marching away". [departure / 撤退]

## English
- In 1293, the Mongols departed Java after their invasion against King Kertanegara of Singhasari failed.
- The failed campaign revealed the resilience of local Southeast Asian kingdoms against foreign naval powers.
- This event stands as a reminder that geographic distance and local knowledge can defeat even the largest empires.

## Chinese
- 1293年，蒙古人在入侵爪哇的辛加沙里王国失败后撤离了该地区。
- 这场失败的战役显示了东南亚当地王国抵御外来海上强权的韧性。
- 这一事件提醒人们，地理距离和本地知识甚至可以击败最强大的帝国。
