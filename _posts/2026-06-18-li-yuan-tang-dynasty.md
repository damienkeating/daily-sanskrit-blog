**Daily Sanskrit learning - history's today: 18/06/618**

**Today's theme:** Empire and Dynasty / 帝国与王朝

## Sanskrit news
- संवत् ६१८ तमे वर्षे जून मासस्य १८ दिनाङ्के लि-युआनः ताङ्-वंशस्य सम्राट् अभवत्।
- तेन चीनस्य शासनम् आरब्धं, येन त्रिशतं वर्षाणि सुस्थिरं राज्यं बभूव।
- अस्य राज्यारम्भः चीनीतिहासस्य महत्त्वपूर्णं अङ्गम् अभवत्।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Empire and Dynasty
- **सम्राट्** (samrāṭ) — emperor, universal ruler; literally "one who reigns over all". emperor / 皇帝
- **वंशः** (vaṃśaḥ) — dynasty, lineage; literally "bamboo, that which continues in a line". dynasty / 朝代
- **शासनम्** (śāsanam) — government, rule; literally "instruction, direction". rule / 统治

## English
- On June 18, 618, Li Yuan proclaimed himself Emperor Gaozu, founding the Tang dynasty in China.
- His rule initiated three centuries of prosperous and culturally brilliant imperial governance.
- The Tang dynasty became one of the most glorious chapters in Chinese and world history.

## Chinese
- 公元618年6月18日，李渊称帝，建立唐朝，是为唐高祖。
- 他的统治开启了中国近三百年的盛世，文化灿烂，国力强盛。
- 唐朝成为中国乃至世界历史上最辉煌的王朝之一。
