Daily Sanskrit learning — history's today: 23/06/229
Today's theme: Sovereignty / 皇权

## Sanskrit news
- संवत्सरे २२९, सुन्-च्वान् पूर्वीय-वू-राज्यस्य सम्राट् इति स्वं घोषयामास। (In the year 229, Sun Quan proclaimed himself emperor of Eastern Wu.)
- तेन नवीनं राज्यं स्थापितं, चीनदेशे त्रयाणां राष्ट्राणां मध्ये। (He established a new kingdom, among the Three Kingdoms of China.)
- अस्याभिषेकेन राजनीतिकं सन्तुलनं परिवर्तितम् अभवत्। (By this coronation, the political balance was transformed.)

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Sovereignty / 主权
- सम्राट् (samrāṭ) — emperor; literal: one who rules completely. emperor / 皇帝
- राज्यम् (rājyam) — kingdom, realm; literal: that which is ruled. kingdom / 王国
- अभिषेकः (abhiṣekaḥ) — coronation, consecration; literal: sprinkling of sacred water. coronation / 加冕

## English
- In the year 229, Sun Quan proclaimed himself emperor of Eastern Wu, establishing the Wu kingdom as one of the Three Kingdoms of China.
- This declaration shifted the balance of power among the rival states, solidifying a tripartite division of the empire.
- Sun Quan's ascension demonstrated how military strength and political ambition could forge legitimate imperial authority.

## Chinese
- 公元229年，孙权称帝，建立东吴，正式成为三国时代的一方霸主。
- 这一宣告改变了中国当时的政治格局，使魏、蜀、吴三国鼎立的局面更加稳固。
- 孙权的登基展示了军事才能与政治野心如何结合，从而铸就合法的皇权。
