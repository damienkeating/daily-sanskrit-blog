## Sanskrit news

- संवत्सरे १४२० मासे मे २५ दिनाङ्के हेन्रि नाविगेटर ऑर्डर ऑफ क्राइस्टस्य अधिपतिः नियुक्तः अभवत्।
- एतेन पदेन सः पुर्तगालीयानां समुद्रीयान्वेषणानां मुख्यः संरक्षकः अभवत्।
- तस्य नेतृत्वे युरोपीयाः नूतनमार्गान् अन्वेष्टुं प्रेरिताः अभवन्।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Governance & Leadership

- अधिपतिः (adhipatiḥ) — governor, ruler; literal sense "one who stands over". [governor / 总督]
- नेतृत्वम् (netṛtvam) — leadership; literal sense "the state of being a leader". [leadership / 领导]
- अन्वेषणम् (anveṣaṇam) — exploration, search; literal sense "seeking after". [exploration / 探索]

## English

- On May 25, 1420, Henry the Navigator was appointed governor of the Order of Christ, a powerful Portuguese religious-military order.
- This appointment gave him the resources and authority to sponsor and organize Portugal's early Age of Discovery expeditions along the African coast.
- His leadership laid the foundation for European maritime expansion and transformed global trade routes forever.

## Chinese

- 1420年5月25日，航海家亨利被任命为基督骑士团的总督。
- 这一任命使他获得了资源和权力，能够资助并组织葡萄牙早期的大航海时代探险活动。
- 他的领导为欧洲海上扩张奠定了基础，并永远改变了全球贸易路线。
