## Sanskrit news
- सन् ८७० तमे वर्षे खलीफा अल-मुहतदी विद्रोही सैन्यैः हतः।
- ततः तस्य बान्धवः अल-मुतमिद् खलीफतस्य शासनाधिकारं प्राप्तवान्।
- एषा घटना इस्लामिक-इतिहासे शक्ति-सङ्घर्षस्य तीव्रम् उदाहरणम् अस्ति।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Political Strife and Succession (राजनीतिक-क्षोभः उत्तराधिकारश्च)
- विद्रोहः (vidrohaḥ) — rebellion; revolt against authority. 叛乱 / rebellion
- हत्या (hatyā) — assassination; killing of a person. 暗杀 / assassination
- उत्तराधिकारः (uttarādhikāraḥ) — succession; inheritance of power or title. 继承 / succession

## English
- In 870, Caliph al-Muhtadi was killed by rebellious troops, throwing the Abbasid Caliphate into turmoil.
- His cousin Al-Mu'tamid immediately succeeded him, seizing control amid deepening military unrest.
- The episode illustrates how assassination and forced succession often defined medieval Islamic political life.

## Chinese
- 公元870年，哈里发 al-Muhtadi 被叛乱军队杀害，阿拔斯王朝陷入动荡。
- 他的堂兄 al-Mu'tamid 随即继位，在日益加深的军事动荡中夺取了控制权。
- 这一事件表明，暗杀与强制继承往往决定了中世纪伊斯兰世界的政治格局。
