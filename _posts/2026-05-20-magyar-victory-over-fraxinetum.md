Daily Sanskrit learning - history's today: 20/05/0942
Today's theme: Conflict / War / 冲突战争

Sanskrit news:
- संवत्सरे ९४२ मग्याराणां सेना फ्राक्सिनेतुं आक्रमणं कृतवती। (In the year 942, the army of the Magyars made an attack on Fraxinetum.)
- तत्र भीषणं युद्धं जातं, बहवः वीराः मृताः। (There a terrible battle occurred, many heroes died.)
- अस्य युद्धस्य फलतः युरोपस्य सीमाप्रदेशेषु परिवर्तनं जातम्। (As a result of this war, a change occurred in the border regions of Europe.)

Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Conflict / War
- आक्रमणम् (ākramaṇam) — attack, assault; literal sense: "stepping upon". attack / 袭击
- सेना (senā) — army, armed force; literal sense: "that which is armed". army / 军队
- विजयः (vijayaḥ) — victory, conquest; literal sense: "victorious". victory / 胜利

English
- In 942, a Magyar raiding army defeated the forces of the Muslim frontier state of Fraxinetum.
- This battle exposed the vulnerability of frontier defenses and shifted the balance of power in southern Europe.
- The event reminds us that even small raiding forces can reshape regional politics and territorial control.

Chinese
- 公元942年，马扎尔突袭军队击败了穆斯林边境国家弗拉西内图姆的部队。
- 这场战斗暴露了边境防御的脆弱性，并改变了南欧的力量平衡。
- 这一事件提醒我们，即使是小规模的突袭部队也能重塑地区政治和领土控制。
