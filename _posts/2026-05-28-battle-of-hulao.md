**Daily Sanskrit learning - history's today: 28/05/621**

**Today's theme:** Warfare and Victory / 战争与胜利

## Sanskrit news
- संवत् ६२१ तमे वर्षे मई मासस्य २८ दिनाङ्के हुलाओ-युद्धे ली-शिमिनः दोउ-जियाण्डेः महत्यां सेनां पराजितवान्।
- अनेन विजयेन ताङ्ग-वंशस्य स्थापनाय मार्गः मुक्तः अभवत्।
- सुइ-वंशस्य पतनानन्तरं चीनी-साम्राज्यस्य पुनर्निर्माणार्थं इदं युद्धं निर्णायकम् अभवत्।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Warfare and Victory
- **युद्धम्** (yuddham) — battle, war; literally "fighting, that which is joined". battle / 战争
- **विजयः** (vijayaḥ) — victory; literally "conquest, surpassing". victory / 胜利
- **सेनā** (senā) — army; literally "that which follows the commander". army / 军队

## English
- On May 28, 621, Li Shimin defeated the numerically superior forces of Dou Jiande at the Battle of Hulao.
- This victory decided the civil war following the Sui dynasty's collapse in favor of the Tang dynasty.
- The battle proved that strategic leadership could overcome overwhelming numerical odds.

## Chinese
- 公元621年5月28日，李世民在虎牢关之战中击败了窦建德的庞大军队。
- 这场胜利决定了隋朝政権崩溃后内战的走向，确立了唐朝的统治。
- 此役证明了卓越的战略领导能够战胜压倒性的兵力优势。
