## Sanskrit news
- संवत्सरे १२५७ तमे क्राकोव् नगरं पोलैण्डदेशे नगराधिकारान् प्राप्तवत् ।
- तस्यै नगराय नूतनाः व्यापारनियमाः राज्ञा प्रदत्ताः ।
- एतत् स्थापनं मध्ययूरोपीयवाणिज्यस्य वृद्ध्यै महत् कारणम् अभवत् ।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Urban Governance
- नगरम् (nagaram) — city; lit. "that which does not move". city / 城市
- अधिकारः (adhikāraḥ) — right, authority; lit. "being over, holding". right / 权利
- स्थापनम् (sthāpanam) — establishment, founding; lit. "causing to stand". establishment / 建立

## English
- In 1257, the city of Kraków in Poland received city rights.
- New trade regulations were granted to the city by the king.
- This establishment became a major catalyst for the growth of commerce in Central Europe.

## Chinese
