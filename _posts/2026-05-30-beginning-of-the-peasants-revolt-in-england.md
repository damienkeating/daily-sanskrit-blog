## Sanskrit news
- सं १३८१ तमे वर्षे इङ्ग्लण्डे कृषकाणां विद्रोहः आरब्धः।
- जनता अन्यायपूर्णं करं विरुध्य उत्थिता।
- अस्य विद्रोहस्य इतिहासे महत् महत्त्वम् अस्ति।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Revolt and the Common People
- विद्रोहः (vidrohaḥ) — revolt, rebellion; literally "breaking apart, bursting forth". [revolt / 叛乱]
- जनता (janatā) — the people, masses; literally "collection of the born". [people / 民众]
- मुक्तिः (muktiḥ) — liberation, freedom; literally "release, loosening". [liberation / 解放]

## English
- In 1381, the Peasants' Revolt began in England as commoners rose against oppressive poll taxes.
- The uprising exposed deep social inequalities and challenged the feudal order of medieval England.
- Though eventually suppressed, the revolt became a lasting symbol of resistance against unjust authority.

## Chinese
- 1381年，英国爆发了农民起义，平民反抗沉重的赋税。
- 这次起义揭示了深刻的社会不平等，并挑战了中世纪英格兰的封建秩序。
- 尽管最终被镇压，这场叛乱成为反抗不公正权威的持久象征。
