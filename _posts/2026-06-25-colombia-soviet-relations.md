## Sanskrit news
- सन् १९३५ तमे वर्षे जूनमासस्य पञ्चविंशे दिने कोलम्बिया-सोवियतसङ्घयोः मध्ये सम्बन्धाः स्थापिताः।
- एते सम्बन्धाः दक्षिणअमेरिकायाः यूरोपीयसंघस्य च मध्ये मैत्रीं दर्शयन्ति।
- राजनयेन विश्वे शान्तिः स्थिरा भवति।

## Simple Sanskrit notes for learners — Theme: Diplomacy (राजनयः)
- **सम्बन्धः** (sambandhaḥ) — relation, connection; literally "binding together". relation / 关系
- **मैत्री** (maitrī) — friendship, alliance; literally "friendship". friendship / 友谊
- **राजनयः** (rājanayaḥ) — diplomacy; literally "royal conduct/policy". diplomacy / 外交

## English
- On June 25, 1935, diplomatic relations between Colombia and the Soviet Union were formally established.
- This milestone marked an important bridge between South American and Eastern European international relations during the interwar period.
- The establishment of such ties demonstrates how diplomacy can foster cooperation across vast geographical and ideological distances.
